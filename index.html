Projects

Nowhere Patrol

2017 - 2018, 2025

Nowhere Patrol is a boss rush game with bullet hell and platforming elements. Inspired by classic action arcade games with its steep difficulty curve and non-stop action. The game is a true boss rush. Expect nothing but boss battles, and lots of deaths.

Solo project made in C# with Monogame and FNA.

Chromahertz

2020 - present

Chromahertz is a boss rush bullet hell game about drawing, teleporting, counter attacks, and flowers.

The characters are called "hosts". They live within the Chromahertz hidden in secret channels waiting for lucky guests like you to tune in.

Work in progress.
